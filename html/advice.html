Advice & Tips for Hikers

Practical advice to make your hiking trips safer, more comfortable, and more enjoyable.

Stay Hydrated

Always carry enough water for the hike and take regular sips to prevent dehydration. Water is crucial for energy and concentration.

Wear Appropriate Clothing

Dress in layers so you can adjust to changing weather. Breathable fabrics keep moisture away and help regulate your body temperature.

Plan Your Route

Study the map, know the terrain, and estimate the time needed. Make sure you have a turnaround plan to avoid being caught in darkness.

Leave No Trace

Respect nature by carrying out all trash, avoiding damage to plants, and staying on marked trails to preserve the environment.

Check Weather Conditions

Always check the weather forecast before heading out. Weather can change quickly, especially in mountainous areas.
